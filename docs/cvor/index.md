# CVOR Guide Overview

## What to Expect on Your First Day

Your first cardiac case will most likely be a CABG sternotomy. The room will feel
busy and the setup is more involved than a general OR, but the case flow is highly
predictable once you know the steps. [Write 3–4 sentences here in your own words
about the pace, the team dynamic, the communication expectations, and what surprised
you your first time.]

Before you walk in, make sure you have reviewed:

- [Cardiac Physiology](cardiac_physiology.md) — CVP waveforms, Swan, SVO2 algorithm
