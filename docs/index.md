# CVOR Anesthesia Reference

A clinical reference for CRNA students rotating through cardiac anesthesia. You will also find lots of other reference material relating to board review, specific general case pearls of wisdom, general case complications to be aware of, and lots of great supporting references throughout. If it's your very first day in the CVOR please read this [overview](cvor/index.md). Then please proceed to the CVOR Guide below.
